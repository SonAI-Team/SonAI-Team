# 🚀 SonAI Team

Welcome to the official repository of the **SonAI-Team**. We are a group of developers and
researchers passionate about technology, artificial intelligence, and digital well-being. 🌟

## 🎯 Our Mission

Our goal is to create technological solutions that help people regain their focus in an increasingly
noisy and distracted world. We believe that the integration of hardware (Mobile and Wearable) and
intelligent software can significantly improve the quality of daily life. ✨

## 🛠️ Our Projects

We maintain two primary open-source projects that embody our philosophy:

- **[SonAI](https://github.com/SonAI-Team/SonAI)**: 🎧 An intelligent focus zone for Mobile and Wear
  OS
  using real-time environment analysis and procedural sound synthesis.
- **[ssiv](https://github.com/SonAI-Team/ssiv)**: 🖼️ A high-performance subsampling scale image view
  for Android, designed for massive images and zero-lag rendering.

## 🔍 What We Do

### 🎤 1. Applied Audio AI

We leverage **MediaPipe's YAMNet** model for real-time sound classification directly on-device.
🧠 Our systems identify ambient triggers—such as speech, traffic, or machinery—and dynamically adapt
masking levels to ensure maximum privacy and concentration without ever recording or transmitting
audio data.

### 🎹 2. Stochastic Sound Synthesis

We have developed a custom **Stochastic Audio Engine** in Kotlin that generates infinite,
non-repeating soundscapes. 🌊

- **High-Fidelity**: 🔊 Studio-grade **Stereo 48kHz 32-bit Float** output.
- **Organic Drift**: 🍃 Utilizes **Triple-LFO interaction** and turbulence modulation to prevent
  auditory fatigue common with static loops.
- **Atmospheric Modes**: 🌌 Deep Space, 🌬️ Stellar Wind, 🌍 Earth Rumble, and procedural Rain/Ocean
  environments.
- **Binaural Beats**: 🧠 Integrated Alpha, Beta, Theta, and Delta wave overlays for targeted mental
  states.

### ⌚ 3. Wearable Development & Biofeedback

We are experts in **Wear OS** development, utilizing the latest **Material 3 Expressive** and
**ProtoLayout** APIs. ⌚

- **Biofeedback**: 💓 Integration with Health Services to monitor heart rate and stress, dynamically
  adjusting audio masking in real-time.
- **Bidirectional Sync**: 🔄 Seamless real-time state synchronization between mobile and wearable
  devices.

### 🤖 4. Modern Android Automation

Our applications are built for the future of Android (SDK 34-37), focusing on: 📱

- **Geofencing**: 📍 Location-aware automation for office or library "Focus Zones".
- **Health Connect**: 🏥 Secure integration of well-being and health data.
- **Auto-DND**: 🌙 Intelligent management of "Do Not Disturb" modes via system policy access.

### 🖼️ 5. High-Performance Imaging & UI

Performance is at the core of our UI philosophy. ⚡ Our **ssiv (Subsampling Scale Image View)**
library
uses a **two-tier loading strategy** (Base Layer + Tile Layer) and **Kotlin Coroutines** to display
ultra-HD maps and blueprints with minimal memory overhead and zero `OutOfMemoryError` risk. 🚀

## 🛡️ Our Philosophy: Privacy First

Everything we do puts user privacy at the center. 🔐 Microphone analysis occurs exclusively locally
and
in volatile memory; no audio data is ever recorded, stored, or transmitted. All our tools are fully
compliant with Android 14+ foreground service and data attribution requirements. ✅
